# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to fostering an open, welcoming, and inclusive environment for everyone participating in Sinnamon.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Give and receive feedback professionally.
- Assume good intent, and ask clarifying questions when unsure.
- Focus on improving the product and helping teammates succeed.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination of any kind.
- Personal attacks, insults, or abusive language.
- Deliberate disruption of discussions, reviews, or collaboration.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, reviews, discussions, and other community channels.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it to the maintainers through the project’s issue tracker or designated maintainer contact channels.

## Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct. They may take appropriate corrective action in response to behavior that violates these standards.

## Enforcement Guidelines

Possible enforcement actions may include:

- Request for behavior change
- Warning
- Temporary restriction from participation
- Permanent removal from project spaces
